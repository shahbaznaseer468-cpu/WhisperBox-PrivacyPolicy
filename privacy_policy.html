Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

Welcome to WhisperBox. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better user experience, verify accounts, and maintain security. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you sign up using an Email and Password or via Google Sign-In, we collect your email address, profile picture (if applicable), and display name.
  • App Content & Interactions: We save details regarding the novels you write, bookmarks, reading progress, and coin balances on our database.
  • Device Information: We may collect standard operational logs (like crash analytics) to improve app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate accounts and secure user access.
  • To maintain and update your library, coins balance, and written novels.
  • To facilitate the in-app economy (coin purchases, unlock history, and writer payouts).
  • To deliver relevant advertisements and display notifications.

3. Third-Party Services & Analytics

Our app incorporates third-party Software Development Kits (SDKs) provided by Google to handle critical backend functionalities. These include:

  • Firebase Authentication: Used for secure user sign-in and session management.
  • Cloud Firestore & Firebase Storage: Used to host and sync app data, including user profiles, written novels, chapters, and comments.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device advertising IDs to serve personalized ads.

4. Data Retention and Account Deletion

We retain your personal data as long as your account is active. We are committed to giving you full control over your data. You can request to delete your account and personal data in the following ways:

  • In-App Deletion: You can delete your account immediately inside the app by going to your Profile -> Settings -> Delete Account and confirming. This permanently removes your profile, coin balances, and personal records from our databases.
  • Web / Email Request: You can email us at shahbaznaseer468@gmail.com with your registered email address, and we will delete your account and associated data manually within 7 business days.

5. Children's Privacy

WhisperBox does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately from our servers.
